F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Care and how does it work?

AI Care is an AI-powered healthcare platform that uses advanced image recognition and natural language processing to provide accurate diagnostics, streamline workflows, and offer real-time insights for healthcare professionals and patients.

How accurate are the AI-driven diagnostics?

AI Care uses state-of-the-art algorithms for image analysis, achieving high accuracy rates comparable to experienced medical professionals. However, it’s always recommended that healthcare professionals review the results for final diagnosis.

Can AI Care be used in remote or underserved areas?

Yes! AI Care is designed to be accessible in remote areas. It provides powerful diagnostic capabilities even without extensive medical infrastructure, helping healthcare providers deliver quality care where it's needed most.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer dedicated customer support through email, phone, and chat. Premium users also have access to 24/7 support with priority assistance for urgent matters.

Is AI Care suitable for small clinics or large hospitals?

AI Care is flexible and scalable. It’s ideal for small clinics, offering essential diagnostics, and equally powerful for large hospitals where it integrates with complex systems to improve efficiency and patient outcomes.
